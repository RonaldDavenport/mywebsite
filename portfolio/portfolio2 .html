Ai Email Generator

API's Used : Klaviyo and Open AI

Description : This app allows users to quickly create and customize email campaigns using pre-built templates and their own input. The app integrates with Klaviyo, a popular email marketing platform, to send out the campaigns directly. Users can choose from various form inputs to customize the email content and design, and preview and edit the HTML code before sending. I created this app to help streamline my current position as an Email Marketer. The app is in it's infant stages, as I have a lot of features left to implement.
